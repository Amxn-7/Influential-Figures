Modern Leaders of India

Narendra Modi

As the current Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi has introduced significant reforms in economic policies, digital innovation, and governance. His leadership has positioned India as a growing global power.

D.Y. Chandrachud

Justice D.Y. Chandrachud is the Chief Justice of India, known for his landmark judgments on human rights, gender equality, and privacy. His judicial work has had a profound impact on modern Indian law and governance.

Sundar Pichai

The CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC, Sundar Pichai, is recognized for his leadership in shaping the world’s most popular search engine and for driving innovation in technology globally.

Kiran Bedi

India’s first woman to join the officer ranks of the Indian Police Service, Kiran Bedi has been an advocate for justice and reform. She has held various leadership roles in government and civil society.

Satya Nadella

As the CEO of Microsoft, Satya Nadella has transformed the company, focusing on cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and digital transformation. His leadership has significantly impacted global tech development.
